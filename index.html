KeyInvest

Risk Learning Portal

A short series of online modules that help every staff member understand how KeyInvest manages risk, how to recognise and report Incidents, and how to raise Issues — anchored to our Enterprise-wide Risk Management Framework and APRA CPS 220, 230 and 234.

All staff ~30 minutes total CPS 220 / 230 / 234 Evidence captured
Welcome — what to expect
Each module takes around 10 minutes and follows the same pattern: a short concept section, four realistic KeyInvest scenarios with feedback, and a completion declaration that is recorded for APRA reporting purposes. You can complete the modules in any order, but we recommend working through them in sequence — each builds on the one before. Your completion is recorded automatically.
